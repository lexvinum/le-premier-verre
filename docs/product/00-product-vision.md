# Product Vision — Le Premier Verre

# Mission

Le Premier Verre helps people discover, understand, remember and enjoy wine.

Our goal is not simply to catalog wines.

We want to become the most intelligent and beautiful wine companion available.

---

# Vision

Every bottle has a story.

Every tasting deserves to be remembered.

Le Premier Verre combines technology, design and artificial intelligence to create the best personal wine experience.

---

# Target Users

## Beginners

People starting to explore wine.

Needs:

- understand wine
- learn vocabulary
- receive recommendations
- remember favorites

---

## Enthusiasts

Wine lovers building a cellar.

Needs:

- tasting history
- cellar management
- food pairing
- aging recommendations

---

## Professionals

Sommeliers, restaurants and wine educators.

Needs:

- professional tasting notes
- curated collections
- event preparation
- client recommendations

---
